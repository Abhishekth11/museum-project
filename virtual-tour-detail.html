About This Tour

Our Modern Art Gallery features works from the early 20th century to contemporary pieces. The collection includes paintings, sculptures, and installations from renowned artists who have shaped the course of modern art history.

This virtual tour allows you to navigate through the gallery spaces at your own pace, with the ability to zoom in on artworks for detailed viewing and access information about each piece and its creator.
