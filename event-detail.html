Guided Tour: Modern Masterpieces

December 15, 2023 | 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM

About This Event

Join our expert curator for a guided tour of our Modern Masterpieces exhibition. This tour will provide in-depth insights into the artworks, their historical context, and the artists' techniques.

The tour will last approximately 60 minutes and is suitable for all ages and knowledge levels. Our curator will be happy to answer any questions you may have about the exhibition or the artworks.
